What’s inside each set

Every doll is a 4-page A4 PDF: a cover, plus standing, sitting, and sleeping poses — each page with three watercolor outfits to cut out and swap.

How to print & play

1Print on A4 or Letter paper at 100% (actual size).
2Cut out the doll and outfits along the dotted lines.
3Fold the little stand at the bottom so the doll stands up.
4Play! Mix and match outfits and make up stories.

💡 Tip: glue the pages onto cardstock before cutting to make the dolls sturdier and last much longer.
