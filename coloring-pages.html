What’s inside each set

Every character comes as a 3-page A4 PDF — standing, sitting, and sleeping. Each page has clean line art to color in, plus three color ideas at the top to copy or mix.

How to print & color

1Print on A4 or Letter paper at 100% (actual size).
2Pick colors — copy the ideas at the top, or invent your own.
3Color it in! Crayons, colored pencils, or markers all work.
4Show it off — pin it up, or cut it out to play with.

💡 Tip: print on slightly thicker paper so markers don’t bleed through, and the finished pages stay nice and flat.
